### CAUSE AND EFFECT RELATIONSHIPS

<dl>
  <dt><b>cause – вызывать</b></dt>
  <dd><em>e.g.</em> An increase in pressure <strong>causes</strong> a shift in chemical equilibrium towards the reaction proceeding with a decrease in volume. </dd>
  <dt><b>affect / have effect on – иметь влияние на</b></dt>
  <dd><em>e.g.</em> An increase in the concentration of reacting substances <strong>affects</strong> the shift of the equilibrium of reversible reaction towards the reaction product.</dd>
  <dt><b>influence / have influence on - иметь влияние на</b></dt>
  <dd><em>e.g.</em>  Pressure, concentration, and temperature <strong>influence</strong> the shift of equilibrium in a reversable reaction towards decreasing this effect.</dd>
  <dt><b>result in – приводить к</b></dt>
  <dd><em>e.g.</em> The shift of chemical equilibrium towards an increase in gaseous molecules <strong>results in</strong> a decrease in pressure. </dd>
  <dt><b>lead to – вести к</b></dt>
  <dd><em>e.g.</em> An increase in temperature in an exothermic reaction in equilibrium <strong>leads to</strong> a shift in equilibrium towards the reactants. </dd>
</dl>
