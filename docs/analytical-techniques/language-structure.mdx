## DESCRIBING PROCESSES (REVIEW)

<table>
  <tr><th>Active voice</th><th>Passive voice</th></tr>
  <tr>
    <td>
      When the subject performs an action, it is active voice.
    </td>
    <td>
      When the subject is acted on by the verb, it is passive voice.
    </td>
  </tr>
  <tr><th colspan='2'>Compare:</th></tr>
  <tr>
    <td>
      Brazil grows coffee.<br/>Coffee grows Brazil. <strong>(wrong)</strong>
    </td>
    <td>
      Coffee is grown in Brazil. <strong>(right)</strong><br/>Subject + be + V<sub>3/ed</sub>
    </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td>
      <em>e.g.</em> <u>Halogenoalkanes undergo</u> elimination reactions when heated with ethanolic sodium hydroxide, forming alkenes.
    </td>
    <td>
      <em>e.g.</em> <u>The C-I bond <strong>is broken</strong></u> during its reactions.(распадается).<br/> 
      <em>e.g.</em> <u>Halogenoalkanes <strong>are attacked</strong></u> by nucleophiles.(атакуются).<br/> 
      <em>e.g.</em> If <u>one or more of the hydrogen atoms</u> in an alkane molecule <u><strong>are replaced</strong></u> by halogen atoms, the compound is called a halogenoalkane. (заменяются)
    </td>
  </tr>
</table>
