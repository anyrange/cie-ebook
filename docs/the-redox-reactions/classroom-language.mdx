## Elaborating correct responses from students: <AudioButton url="/media/Elaborating correct responses from students.0f061d83.mp3" />

- You are right. Can you tell me more?
- Yes, that’s right. What else do you about that?
- You are correct. How did you lean that?
- Can you also tell me why this (concept, information) is important?
- Good thinking!

## Asking for clarification: <AudioButton url="/media/Asking for clarification.27fde7ca.mp3" />

- I’m sorry! Was that electron or electrode?
- Pardon me, Amir. Did you say electron or electrode?
- Sorry! I didn’t catch it. What did you say?
- Excuse me. Did you say electrode?
- May I interrupt a moment? Was that electrode?
- What do you mean by...?
- Can you elaborate more on that?
- Sorry. Did you mean...?
