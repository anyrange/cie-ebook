## PRESENT CONTINUOUS PASSIVE

When the process is described **<u>at the moment of happening</u>, the present continous passive** is used.

<table>
  <tr>
    <td rowspan='2'><img src={reactions} width='400' /></td>
    <td>Wrong ❌</td>
    <td>Right ✔</td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td>You are pouring 200 ml of distilled water in a beaker.</td>
    <td>200 ml of distilled water is being poured in a beaker.</td>
  </tr>
</table>
