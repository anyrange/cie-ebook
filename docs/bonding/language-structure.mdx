<dl>
  <dt>
    <strong>If...</strong> – Если ...
  </dt>
  <dd>
    <em>e.g.</em> If one molecule contains hydrogen and the other has fluorine
    nitrogen or oxygen, then they have hydrogen bonding.
  </dd>
  <dt>
    <strong>When ...</strong> – Когда ...
  </dt>
  <dd>
    <em>e.g.</em> When central atom has 2 bonding pairs of electrons and 3
    non-bonding pairs of electrons then it has linear shape of molecule.
  </dd>
  <dt>
    <strong>Unless ... </strong> – Пока не ...
  </dt>
  <dd>
    <em>e.g.</em> Unless chloride of sodium melts, then it does not conduct
    electric current.
  </dd>
</dl>
